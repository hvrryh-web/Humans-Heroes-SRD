Heavy

Effect: Growth without Strength, Stamina, or size bonuses • Cost: 3 points per rank

This is Growth for mass only. This is useful for resisting Trip attempts for example: you get a +1 to resist Trip attempts per mass rank increase.

Extras

Immovable: The mass you gain is unaffected by gravity (thus your weight doesn't increase). Anything based on your mass still applies such as being harder to move or trip. +1 cost per rank.
